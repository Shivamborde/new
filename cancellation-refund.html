Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Cancellation timelines matter. Refunds are processed based on when you cancel.

Cancellation Timeline & Refund Amount

Cancellation Period Refund Amount Processing Time
30+ days before tour 90% refund 5-7 working days
15-29 days before tour 75% refund 5-7 working days
7-14 days before tour 50% refund 7-10 working days
3-6 days before tour 25% refund 7-10 working days
Less than 48 hours No refund -
No-show No refund -

Note: All refunds are processed to the original payment method (Razorpay, card, UPI, or net banking).

Vehicle Booking Cancellation

  • 48+ hours before pickup: 100% refund (minus 5% transaction fee)
  • 24-48 hours before pickup: 50% refund
  • Less than 24 hours: No refund
  • Driver waiting time: First 30 minutes free, then ₹100-300 per hour

Hotel Booking Cancellation

  • 7+ days before check-in: 100% refund (minus 5% transaction fee)
  • 3-6 days before check-in: 50% refund
  • Less than 48 hours: No refund
  • Early check-out: No refund for unused nights

Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Peak season bookings (Diwali, New Year, Christmas, etc.)
  • Special event bookings (festivals, weddings, etc.)
  • Customized tour packages with special arrangements
  • Advance train/flight tickets purchased on your behalf
  • Entry fees to monuments and attractions
  • Permits for wildlife sanctuaries and national parks

Weather-Related Cancellations

  • If we cancel due to extreme weather: 100% refund or reschedule
  • If government issues travel advisory: 100% refund
  • If natural calamity occurs: 100% refund
  • If you cancel due to weather forecast: Standard policy applies

Medical Emergency Cancellations

In case of medical emergencies (with valid medical certificates):

  • 100% refund minus 10% processing fee if canceled before tour start
  • Valid medical certificate from registered practitioner required
  • Hospitalization proof may be requested
  • Refund processed within 7-10 working days

Date Changes / Rescheduling

  • Free reschedule: 15+ days before tour (once only)
  • ₹500 fee: 7-14 days before tour
  • ₹1000 fee: 3-6 days before tour
  • Not allowed: Less than 48 hours before tour
  • Rescheduled date must be within 6 months of original booking

Refund Processing Details

  • Razorpay (Card/UPI/NetBanking): 5-7 working days
  • Credit Card: 7-10 working days (depends on bank)
  • Debit Card: 7-10 working days
  • UPI: 3-5 working days
  • International Cards: 10-15 working days

Important: Transaction fees charged by payment gateways (Razorpay) are non-refundable even in case of cancellations. This is typically 2-3% of the transaction amount.
