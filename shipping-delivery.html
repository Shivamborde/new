Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

For service-based businesses: This policy explains how we deliver our travel services, not physical products.

What We "Deliver"

As a travel service provider, we deliver the following:

  • Booking Confirmations: Email and SMS immediately after payment
  • Tour Itineraries: Detailed day-by-day plans sent to your email
  • Vehicle Services: Physical delivery of vehicles at your location
  • Hotel Vouchers: Digital vouchers for hotel check-in
  • E-Tickets: Digital tickets for monuments and attractions

Digital Delivery (Booking Confirmations)

Immediate Delivery (After Payment)

  • Instant email confirmation with booking ID
  • SMS confirmation to registered mobile number
  • WhatsApp message with booking summary
  • Digital invoice for your records

24-48 Hours Before Tour

  • Detailed itinerary with timings and meeting points
  • Driver/guide contact information
  • Weather forecast and packing suggestions
  • Emergency contact numbers

Physical Delivery (Vehicle Services)

Vehicle Pickup Locations

  • Airport Pickup: Driver will meet you at arrival gate with name board
  • Railway Station: Driver will wait at platform exit
  • Hotel Pickup: Driver will wait at hotel lobby/reception
  • Home Pickup: Driver will arrive at your specified address

Vehicle Delivery Timelines

Service Type Delivery Time Confirmation
Airport/Railway Pickup 15-30 mins before arrival SMS with driver details 2 hours prior
Hotel Pickup 15 mins before scheduled time Call from driver 30 mins prior
Home Pickup At scheduled time Driver shares live location
Multi-day Tour Each day as per itinerary Previous night reminder

Delivery Areas & Coverage

  • Maharashtra State: Full coverage including all major cities
  • Airport Transfers: Chh. Sambhajinagar, Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur
  • Railway Stations: All major railway stations in Maharashtra
  • Tourist Destinations: Ajanta, Ellora, Mahabaleshwar, Lonavala, etc.
  • Inter-state: Available on request (additional permits required)

Delivery Notifications

You'll receive updates at every stage:

  • Immediate: Booking confirmation (Email + SMS)
  • 24 hours before: Reminder with driver details
  • 2 hours before: Live tracking link (WhatsApp)
  • 30 minutes before: Driver calling you
  • At pickup: "Arrived" notification
  • After drop: Trip completion confirmation

Delivery Delays

  • Traffic Delays: Driver will inform you immediately
  • Flight/Train Delays: We track your arrival and adjust pickup time
  • Vehicle Issues: Alternative vehicle arranged within 30 minutes
  • Compensation: ₹100 per hour for delays beyond 30 minutes (excluding traffic)

What You Need to Receive Delivery

  • Valid ID proof (Aadhar, Passport, Driver's License, Voter ID)
  • Booking confirmation (digital copy on phone is sufficient)
  • Working phone number for driver communication
  • Exact pickup location with landmarks (for home pickup)

Delivery of Payment Receipts

  • Payment Receipt: Instant email after successful Razorpay payment
  • Tax Invoice: Sent within 24 hours of booking completion
  • GST Invoice: Available on request (provide GST number at booking)
  • Refund Confirmation: Email when refund is processed

Tour Package Delivery

What's Included:

  • Vehicle with chauffeur for entire duration
  • Accommodation as per package (hotel vouchers)
  • Meals where specified (meal coupons if applicable)
  • Entry tickets to monuments (delivered digitally)
  • Guide services (meet at designated points)

Express Delivery (Urgent Bookings)

  • Within 2 hours: Instant confirmation and driver assignment
  • Within 4 hours: Vehicle delivered to your location
  • Express Fee: ₹500 extra (waived for premium packages)
  • Availability: Subject to vehicle availability

Failed Delivery / Missed Pickup

  • If you miss pickup: Driver waits 30 minutes, then cancellation with no refund
  • If we fail to deliver: Full refund + 100% compensation
  • Wrong location provided: Additional charges for extra distance
